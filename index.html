How the Cup works
Teams

Every player goes on one of two wheels — forwards and defenders. Each spin pairs one from each wheel, and that pairing is your team for the whole cup. The wheel order is random, and nobody is drafted twice.

Group matches
  • Both scores are entered, and the higher score wins.
  • There are no draws — equal scores are rejected, not recorded.
  • A match counts for nothing until both scores are in.
The table

There is no points system. Teams are ranked directly by results:

  • P — matches played  ·  W — matches won
  • GD — goal difference (goals scored minus conceded)
  • GF — goals scored

Order is decided by wins first, then GD, then GF. If two teams are still exactly level, whoever won the match between them goes above. The highlighted rows are the ones who go through — the top two of each group, or the top four when all five teams share a single group.

Format
  • 2 teams — no group stage at all, straight to the Grand Final.
  • 3 or 4 teams — one group, everyone plays everyone once, top two meet in the Grand Final.
  • 5 teams — one group, top four reach the Semifinals, seeded 1 v 4 and 2 v 3.
  • 6 or more — two groups, round robin inside each, top two from each reach the Semifinals.

With two groups the Semifinals are crossed, so group winners avoid each other until the final: A1 v B2 and B1 v A2.

Suggesting a score

Played a group match while the admin wasn't around? Sign in with any Google account and type the score straight into that match — it's sent as a suggestion, shown to everyone with your name on it. It doesn't change the standings; the admin accepts it (or corrects it) when they're back. Only matches with no score yet can be suggested.

When the knockout starts

The Start Knockout button appears only once every group match has a score — no partial brackets. The admin starts it, and from that point the group scores are locked, so the table behind the bracket can't shift.

Deciding a knockout match
  • Knockout games are not scored — the admin taps the winning team.
  • Tapping the same team again undoes the pick.
  • Changing an earlier round clears everything after it, since those matchups no longer exist.

Winning the Grand Final takes the cup, and that team is added to Past Champions the moment the final is decided.
